DJ Booth

As the DJ Booth is upgraded, its range increases and its discounts become more impactful, allowing players to cover larger portions of the map with its benefits. One of its most important upgrades also grants a small range boost to nearby towers, helping other units reach enemies earlier and stay effective longer. The DJ is a cornerstone of many strategies, particularly in longer or harder modes where managing money and maximizing tower efficiency is crucial. While it doesn’t deal damage, its economic and utility advantages make it one of the most valuable support towers in the game, often paired with Commander for a well-rounded, high-performance defense.

Comander

As the Commander is upgraded, its ability becomes more effective, with longer duration and shorter cooldowns, allowing players to maintain near-constant buffs if used strategically. Skilled players often chain multiple Commanders together to keep the fire rate boost active almost nonstop, significantly increasing their defense’s overall efficiency. While it doesn’t contribute direct damage, the Commander is considered one of the most valuable towers in the game due to its ability to amplify the strength of every other unit. It’s a key part of many advanced strategies and is especially important in late-game scenarios where every bit of extra damage can make the difference between victory and defeat.

Scout

As the Scout is upgraded, its attack speed increases and it gains the ability to handle slightly tougher enemies, though it still falls off in later waves compared to more advanced towers. Despite this, its efficiency and reliability make it a staple choice for beginners and a dependable support option for experienced players during early stages. Scouts are all about smart placement and numbers—strength in simplicity.

History

  • Released by Paradoxum Games on Roblox Very simple game with basic towers and maps
  • Growth (2019–2020) Gained popularity through co-op gameplay Early events and harder modes brought more players
  • Big overhaul (Mega Update) changed graphics and systems Hardcore mode added, making the game more challenging
  • Seasonal events became a core feature Exclusive towers and bosses increased hype
  • More polished, balanced, and competitive Large community with constant updates and evolving meta
